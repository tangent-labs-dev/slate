Notes that stay fast and local.

Slate is a local-first markdown notes app with wiki links, backlinks, and smart note navigation. Your notes stay on your device and work offline by default.

Features

Why choose Slate?

Built for focused writing with practical knowledge-management features.

code

Markdown + Wiki Links

Write in plain markdown and connect notes instantly with Obsidian-style wiki links like [[Project Plan]].

cloud_sync

Local-First Storage

Notes are saved in your browser with IndexedDB, so the app starts fast and works even without an internet connection.

gpp_good

Backlinks and Smart Rename

See linked mentions automatically and keep references intact with automatic wiki-link updates when renaming notes.
